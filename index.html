\Services

What I can offer

Exterior Visualisation

I specialize in creating photorealistic exterior renderings that highlight your design's relationship with its environment, materials, and lighting. With hands-on experience in an architecture office, I can interpret and work with architectural drawings from full projects or simple sketches.

Interior Visualisation

I specialize in realistic interior visualizations, transforming layouts and technical drawings into vivid renderings. My background in interior architecture, combined with hands-on experience in an architecture office, allows me to bring designs to life with precision, capturing light, texture, and atmosphere.

Website Development

I design and develop websites that balance functionality and visual appeal. My design background, combined with my studies in applied computer science, allows me to create digital experiences that are both user-friendly and intuitive. A well-crafted website can elevate your project or portfolio, making a strong impression on clients.

Print Design

I design impactful print materials that clearly communicate your project’s message. I have designed booklets, presentation boards, and brochures for architects and designers. With experience in design, I focus on creating balanced compositions. Using the right colors, typography, and layout, I ensure visual harmony and effective communication.

\About

About

I'm Karl Kaln, and I specialize in high-quality architectural renderings. My passion for this craft began during my interior architecture studies in Latvia and was further enriched by 5 years of hands-on experience working in an architecture office.

Now based in Geel, Belgium, I’m pursuing a degree in Applied Computer Science at Thomas More University of Applied Sciences, with a focus on Application Development, Web Design, and Digital Projects. I thrive on merging my creative background with technical expertise to bring ideas to life.
